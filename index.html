Welcome to my website

Croeso or Welcome to all visitors to my recently revamped website.

It's primary purpose is to publish the family tree information I have collected over the last decade or so. The primary surnames I am researching are:-
Mudford, Evans, Stone, Lloyd, Walters, Francis, Hillier, Adlam, Price, Lewis, Davies, Minty, Maggs, Silcox and Llewelyn.

A secondary aim is to promote sites that promote Wales and all things Welsh including the language.

The new features are My Blog and My Picture Gallery. I hope to keep the site updated and improved on a regular basis. Thank you for taking the time to visit this site. I hope it has been of use or interest or both.
