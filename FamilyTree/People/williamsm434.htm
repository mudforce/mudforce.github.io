Martha Williams Biography

Martha Williams was born on 26th May 1845 in Abercanaid. Her father was Lewis Williams, a coal miner and her mother was Cesiah Williams formerly Llewelyn. Cesiah registered the birth on 23rd June 1845 by making her mark.

She married Evan Evans on 12th Nov 1865 in Zoar Chapel, Merthyr Tydfil. He was a 21-year-old coalminer. She was a 21 yr old. Both were resident in Abercanaid. His father was Evan Evans, a coalminer. Her father was Lewis Williams, a coalminer. The witnesses were Lewis Williams and Thomas Evans, presumably his brother. (GRO Ref 11a 661)
