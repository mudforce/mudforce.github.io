Matilda Plummer Biography

Born 1846 and baptised 10 May 1846 in Coleford, Somerset, England
Died: Q4 1882 and buried 7 October 1882 in St. Mary's, Saltford, Somerset, England

Married John Ruddock 25 December 1867 in St. John the Baptist, Frome, Somerset, England. Witness were Fred Plummer (brother) and Maria Targett (step mother). Her address Naish Street, his address Friggle Street.

Children: Sydney Albert Ruddock, Sarah Ruddock, Ellen M Ruddock, Emily Sarah Ruddock, Alice Mary Ruddock, Florence Caroline Ruddock
