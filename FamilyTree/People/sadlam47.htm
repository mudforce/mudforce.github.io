Sarah Adlam Biography

She was born in 1827 and baptised 20th May 1827 in Wookey, nr Wells, Somerset, England. Her father was James Adlam and her mother Ann Tucker.

She married Philip Alfred Stone in the Parish Church of Temple in the County of Bristol on 19th July 1858. He was a 28-year-old labourer. She was aged 29. Both had Temple as their place of residence. His father was Gabriel Stone, a butcher. Her father was James Adlam, a farmer. Witnesses George and Elizabeth Bush?. Marriage GRO ref 6a 109.

She died on 4th January 1902 in 32 Sherbourne Road, Yeovil, Somerset, England. Cause of death is given as Morbus Cordis which is heart disease.
