Maria Hillier

Born: 1835 and baptised 6 December 1835 in Ss Peter and Paul Church, Kilmersdon, Somerset, England
Died: Q4 1910 in Frome District, Somerset, England. (5c 277)

Spouse: John Hale married 7 April 1855 in Ss Peter and Paul Church, Kilmersdon, Somerset, England. (Her name transcribed as Hillyar)
Children: Emily Hale

Maria's Heritage
Parents: Giles Hillier and Eliza Hancox
Siblings: George James Hillier, Hannah Hillier, Edmund Hillier, James Hillier, Hannah Hiller
