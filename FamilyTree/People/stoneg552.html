Gabriel Stone Biography

Born: 1794 and baptised 10 June 1794 at St. John the Baptist Church, Pilton, Somerset, England
Died: 1840 and buried 16 January 1840 in St John the Baptist, Pilton, Somerset, England

Gabriel Stone married Eliza Hippesley by Licence on 25 Sept 1820 at Shepton Mallet. Witnesses were Stephen Stone and Harriet Stone. Page 83 Entry 247.S.Mallet Parish Records. A marriage bond had been made dated 23 September 1820 between Gabriel Stone and Edward Hippisley, a sadler.
