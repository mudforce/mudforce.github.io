Dorcas Silcox Biography

Dorcas Silcox was born circa 1788 in Dunkerton, Wellow, Somerset, England. She married Jonathan Minty of Hemington, Somerset, England by banns on 13 Apr 1815 at Foxcote, Somerset, England. Witnesses Betty Smith and Cornelius Rossiter. Page 1 Entry 1 Foxcote PRs. They had 5 children:- James Minty, George Minty, Eliza Minty, Hester Minty and Mary Ann Minty

She died 21st December 1861 in Foxcote, Somerset, England. (1861 Q4 Frome 5c 378)
