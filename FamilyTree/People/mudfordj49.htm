Joseph William Mudford Biography

Born 31st March 1851 (the day after the 1851 census!) in Midsomer Norton sub district Clutton Registration district in the County of Somerset. Father was Thomas Mudford, a labourer and his mother was Eliza Mudford formerly Minty. His mother Eliza on 5th May 1851 registered his birth.

He married Mary Jane Hillier on 17th October 1882 in the Parish Church of Midsomer Norton in the County of Somerset. He was aged 30 and a labourer living in Midsomer Norton. He didn't sign his name but made his mark. She was aged 19 and resident in Midsomer Norton. She signed her name. His father was Thomas Mudford, a labourer and her father, although at first glance is looks like Freddie, was in fact Treddue Hillier, a miner. This looks like the source of the Tredew forename given to one of their sons. The witnesses were George and Martha Hillier.

He and Mary Jane had 6 children :- Arthur Thomas Mudford 18th Dec 1885 - 2nd Apr 1958, Joseph William Mudford born 12th Jul 1888, Ethel Maud Mudford born 10 Sep 1890, George Tredew Mudford 14th Dec 1892 - 15th Mar 1915, Mary Mudford born 29th Mar 1895, Mabel Mudford 17th Nov 1896 (4 months after J Wâ€™s death)- 4th Mar 1988

He died 17th July 1896 in Midsomer Norton in the County of Somerset aged 45. He was described as a farm labourer. Cause of death was Pneumonia pleurisy and Cardiac Syncope. His widow, Mary on 20th July 1896, registered the death.
