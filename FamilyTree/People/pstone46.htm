Philip Alfred Stone Biography

He was born 23rd May 1830 in Shepton Mallet, Somerset, England and baptised 28th July 1833 in Shepton Mallet Parish Church, Somerset, England. His father was Gabriel Stone, a butcher of High Street, Shepton Mallet. His mother was Eliza Hippesley.

He married Sarah Adlam in the Parish Church of Temple in the County of Bristol on 19th July 1858. He was a 28-year-old labourer. She was aged 29. Both had Temple as their place of residence. His father was Gabriel Stone, a butcher. Her father was James Adlam, a farmer. Witnesses George and Elizabeth Bush? Marriage GRO ref 6a 109.

He died on 7th April 1915 in 30 Sherbourne Road, Yeovil of Senile Decay and Syncope. The death was register by his son P A Stone
