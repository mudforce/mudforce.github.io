Rachel Francis Lloyd Biography

Born 24th September 1896 in The Triangle, Pentrebach, Glamorgan, Wales. Died 1976 and buried in Cefn Cemetery, nr Merthyr Tydfil, Glamorgan, Wales. She was a tailoress.
