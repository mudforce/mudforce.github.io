Treddue\Tredhue\Tradua Hillier Biography

On 2nd March 1823, Tarduus Hillier was baptised at SS Peter and Paul, Kilmersdon, his parents being William and Anna Hillier (nee Button). Other children of theirs baptised at Kilmersdon were: -
James Hillier baptised 15 Aug 1813, Giles Hillier baptised 18 Apr 1815, George Hillier baptised 30 Aug 1818, Anna Hillier baptised 28 Aug 1825 and Mary Hillier baptised 13 Jun 1830

A marriage record found was for a Dew Hillyar aged 29. a coalminer who married Eliza Maggs aged 21 servant, on 19 Mar 1854 at Kilmersdon. His father was William, her father Charles. Witnesses were Isaac Maggs and Eliza Maggs.

Baptisms found for children of Dew/Dewy/Dewe & Eliza are: -
Elizabeth Hillier baptised 9 Oct 1859, Sarah Hillier baptised 16 Sep 1863 (buried 13 Aug 1865 aged 2 years), Isaac Hillier baptised 25 Dec 1861, Mary Jane Hillier baptised 5 Apr 1865, Sarah Hillier baptised 1 Sep 1867, Eliza Hillier baptised 28 Dec 1869, Emma Hillier baptised 1st Jan 1872, Albert John Hillier baptised 14 Jun 1874 (buried 19 Jul 1874 aged 2 months), Louisa Maria Hillier baptised 30 May 1875

He died 17th May 1895 in Kilmersdon, Somerset, England. His daughter in law Ellen Hillier registered his death having been present. Cause of death was given as heart failure and chronic bronchitis.
