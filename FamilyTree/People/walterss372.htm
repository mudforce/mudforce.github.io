Sarah Jane Walters Biography

Born: 09 May 1871 (11a 467) in Abercanaid, Glamorgan, Wales. Married Thomas R Thomas on 3rd June 1895 in Merthyr Tydfil Register Office. Had 3 daughters and three sons.

In the 1939 Register at 55 Chapel Street, Abercanaid were: Thomas Rees Thomas (Insurance Agent), Sarah J, Harold T (Shop Manager Boot & Shoes) and Maggie M.

At the same address in the election registers of 1929 and 1930, were Thomas Richard Thomas, Sarah Jane, David Walter, Edward Myrddyn, and Maggie May (showing Richards) as a coming marriage was Q2 1941 (11a 1697) in Merthyr Tydfil.
