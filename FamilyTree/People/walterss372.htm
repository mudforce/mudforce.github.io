Sarah Jane Walters Biography

Born: 09 May 1877 (11a 467) in Abercanaid, Glamorgan, Wales. Married Thomas R Thomas on 3rd June 1895 in Merthyr Tydfil Register Office. Had 3 daughters and three sons.

At the same address in the election registers of 1929 and 1930, were Thomas R Thomas, Sarah Jane, David Walter, Edward Myrddyn, and Maggie May. Maggie May shows as Richards, as a coming marriage was Q2 1941 (11a 1697) in Merthyr Tydfil. Next door in number 56 Chapel Street were Albert and Martha Parte.
