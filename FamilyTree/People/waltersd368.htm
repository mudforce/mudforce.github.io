David Walters Biography

David Walters was born on 4th February 1839 at Tiradda in the parish of Llansadwrn, Carmarthen. His father was John Walters, a farmer. His mother was Margaret Jones. His birth was registered by his father on 9th February 1839. John Walters made his mark rather than signing. He was baptised 16 November 1839 in the parish of Llansadwrn, Carmarthen. Details received on the location are show below: -

TIR-ADDA at SN680324 on map sheet SN63 Height 425 ft. parish of Llansadwrn. 1948 OS map.

David Walters married Elizabeth Lewis on 21st June 1862 in the parish church of Llangadock in Carmarthenshire. His father’s name was John Walters. (Llandovery District 11a 745). She was a spinster of full age. He was a labourer of full age. Her father’s name was Thomas Lewis. His father’s name was John Walters. Her residence at time of marriage was Waunystrad Meiras. His residence at time of marriage was Cwrwaun. The witnesses were James Lewis her younger brother, William Williams, and William Howells.
