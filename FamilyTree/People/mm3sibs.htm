John Evans & Family Biography

John Evans was born 3rd November 1935 in Penyard House, on the hillside above Pentrebach, Merthyr Tydfil, Glamorgan, Wales. He married Rita Matthews (born 6th March 1937) in Ferndale Presbyterian Church, Glamorgan, Wales on 20th August 1960.

He died 14th October 2005 in Prince Charles Hospital, Merthyr Tydfil, Glamorgan, Wales. The funeral service was at St. Fagan's Church, Trecynon followed by interment at Aberdare Cemetery. She died 19th October 2008.

Had two children, Philip and Nicola.

Philip Evans was born 11th July 1965 in Aberdare, Glamorgan, Wales. He married Eiry Morgan (born 1st April 1968) in Cwmdare, nr Aberdare, Glamorgan, Wales in July 1989. They have two sons, Jonathan Evans (born 10th October 1993) and Morgan Lloyd Evans (born 9th August 1996). They are currently living in Guelph, near Toronto in Canada.

Nicola Evans was born 10th March 1971 in Aberdare, Glamorgan, Wales. She married Mark Ringrose (born 25th January 1972) in Aberdare Church, Glamorgan, Wales on 3rd June 1995. They have one son, Joseph John, born 12th January 2000 and one daughter, Emmy Jane, born 1st September 2002
