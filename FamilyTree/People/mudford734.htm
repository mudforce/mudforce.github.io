Emily Mudford Biography

She was born on 17th December 1900 in The Kennels, Stratton on the Fosse, Somerset, England. Fatherâ€™s name on birth certificate is given as Joseph Mudford, a deceased Farm Labourer. Her mother was Mary Jane Mudford formerly Hillier. She registered the birth by making her mark on 9th February 1901. Emily was baptised on 12 June 1902, where her mother's abode is the Union Workhouse.

She died on 13th September 1984 in Cheltenham, Gloucestershire, England.

The 1911 Census has Emily, along with brother Edwin (Edward Short) resident in a Somerset institution as inmates.
