Catherine Price Biography

Born 1874 in Merthyr Tydfil, Glamorgan, Wales. Married David Henry Thomas on 14 August 1900 in Salem Chapel in Thomas Town, Merthyr Tydfil, and had three children, Elfed, Pethion and May. Elfed Thomas had a daughter, Prudence. May Thomas married John Thomas and had a daughter Valerie.
