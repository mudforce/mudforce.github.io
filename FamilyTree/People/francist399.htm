Thomas Francis Biography

Thomas Francis was born on 12th January 1841 in Ditcheat, Somerset in the Evercreech sub district and registration district. His father’s name was Jonas Francis, a labourer. His mother was Charlotte Francis formerly Norman. She registered the birth on 22nd February 1841.

He married Elizabeth Kertion on 24th January 1864 in St. Mary’s Church in the Parish of Bridgewater. She was a 24 yr old servant. He was a 23 yr old engine driver. Her Father was Robert Kertion, a labourer. His father was Jonas Francis, a labourer. The witnesses were John and Fanny Duck. (GRO ref 5C 629.) Both bride and groom signed the register by making their mark.

In the 1911 Census he is a patient in Bridgwater Hospital Age 69 place of birth Ditchette Somerset. He died in Bridgwater on 18th April 1911 in Bridgewater
