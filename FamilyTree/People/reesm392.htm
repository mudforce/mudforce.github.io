Mary Rees Biography

Mary Rees was born c. 1816 in Llangyfelach, Glamorgan, Wales. From the parish records there are four possible baptisms which fit the facts:-

REES Mary Dau of John, Copperman and Catherine 13 Jan 1814 Llangyfelach
REES Mary Dau of Rees Rees, carpenter and Anne Baker 29 Apr 1815 Llangyfelach
REES, Mary dau of Thomas, copperman and Elinor Jenkin 24 Feb 1816 Llangyfelach
REES, Mary dau of William, Tailor and Jennet - 26 Feb 1817 - Morriston
She married Evan Lloyd in the parish church of Saint David & Saint Cyfelach, Llangyfelach on 4th June 1836.

Her eldest daughter Mary is shown as head of the household in the 1861 census so it is presumed she died in the period between the 1851 and 1861 census.
