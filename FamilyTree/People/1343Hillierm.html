Martha Hillier

Born: 1857 and baptised 9 August 1857 in St Peter and Paul, Kilmersdon, Somerset, England
Died: Q3 1931 in Houghton Le Spring, Durham, England (10a 406)

Martha's Family
Spouse: Emmerson Cain married 5 August 1891 in St Peter and Paul, Kilmersdon, Somerset England
Children: Ada Cain, Laura Cain

Martha's Heritage
Parents: Treddue\Tredhue\Tradua Hillier, Eliza Maggs
Siblings: George Hillier, Elizabeth Hillier, Issac Hillier, Sarah Hillier, Mary Jane Hillier, Sarah Hillier, Eliza Hillier, Emm Hillier, Albert John Hillier, Louiza Hillier

Page 179 of the Parish records of St Peter and Paul, Kilmersdon have a marriage on 5 August 1891 of Martha Hiller (b.1857) and widower Emmerson Cain (b,1843) a Colliery Joiner from Durham. Her father was given as Devy Hillier (obs Dewy). Witnesses were George Hiller and Eliza Hillier.
