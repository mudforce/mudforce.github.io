Ernest Philip Albert Stone Biography

Born 1st July 1896 in 49 Town Lane, Shepton Mallet, Somerset, England. Died 2nd September 1969 in Llanelli, West Glamorgan, Wales. Cremated 5th September 1969 in Swansea Crematorium, Wales.

Funeral Announcement in local newspaper.

The funeral took place on Friday, September 5 at Swansea Crematorium of Mr. Ernest Philip Albert Stone, of 55 Coronation Road, Llanelli.

Officiating at the house and crematorium were the Revs. Lawford T. Davies, St. AIbans, and Emlyn Hopkins, Ebenezer.

Mourners were: Messrs. Idris Stone, brother; Frederick Thomas, brother-in-law; Arthur Thomas, John Mudford, Gordon Mudford, Harold Thomas, Howard Thomas, George Thomas, Maldwyn Fryer, John Griffiths, Euros Stephens, Gwyn Davies, nephews; Will Thomas, Vic Roberts, AIbert Sweet, Philip Bradley, friends.

Bearers were: Messrs. A Downing, W. Ross, E. Thomas, S. Oliver, R. Bradley ,E. Hopkins.

Floral tributes from: Sisters and brothers: Vera, George and David, Gladys, Maurice and boys.

Flowers for the house from: Arthur and Bettie, Audrey and Gwyn, Harold, Pam and children, Marion, Haydn and Vera, Joan and Euros, Howard, Doll and Stephen, Sandra and David, Myra, Dave and Wyn, Mary and Will, Enid, Dennis and children, Brenda, Wally and family, Mattie, Les and boys.

Unable to attend owing to illness were: Mr. Stanley Stone, brother, and Hadyn Cole, nephew.

Mr.and Mrs. George Thomas wish to thank the family doctor, Mrs. Myra Thomas, ,Mrs.Grace Bowen , Mrs. Enid Humphries and neighbours of Coronation Road for their many kindnesses and help during their said bereavement.

The funeral arrangements were carried out by Messrs. Lewis and Morgan, Andrew Street.

The 1939 and 1948 electoral registers for Parish of Tidenham, Forest of Dean, Gloucestershire has the following entries:- Albert Clifford Stone and Ada Zanetta Stone (The Cottage, Offas Dyke, Chepstow; Ernest Philip Stone (Beachley Camp, Chesptow); Idris Stone (Offas Dyke Cottage, Woodcroft, Chepstow). Ernest not there in 1956.
