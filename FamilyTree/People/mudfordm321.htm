Mary Mudford Biography

Born 29 March 1895 in Midsomer Norton in the registration district of Clutton in the sub district of Midsomer Norton. Her father was Joseph Mudford, a farm labourer. Her mother was Mary Mudford, formerly Hillier. Mary Jane Mudford registered her birth on 10 May 1895.

She was married on 5 February 1916 at the Register Office in the district of Clutton in the County of Somerset to Edmund Thomas Dennett. He was 27 and a private in the Army Service Corps based at Remount Depot, Shirehampton near Bristol. Her address was Britannia Yard, Chilcompton. Witnesses were Joan & Emma Hillard / Hilland. His father was Edmund Dennett (deceased), a labourer. Her father was Joseph Mudford (deceased), a farm labourer. Dad was not aware of how the Dennett people who Gran wrote to fitted into the family picture until I dug up this information with a little help from Brian Mudford and Greg Donald.

They had 6 children:- Albert G Dennett married Vera Harrington in 1947 and had daughter Yvonne L in 1950.
