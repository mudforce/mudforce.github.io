Mary Edith Lloyd Biography

Mary Edith Lloyd was born 28th December 1894 in The Triangle, Pentrebach, Glamorgan, Wales. She died 1982 in Bridgend, Glamorgan, Wales. She was a milliner.

Married David William Jenkins who was born 11th June 1891, on 16th November 1925 in Ebenezer Chapel, Ebenezer Streeet, Cardiff. David Brinley Evans and Catherine Jane Lloyd were the witnesses. One daughter Gwenfa Jenkins , born 13th May 1926.

In the 1939 residence documents, David and Mary Edith Jenkins are living in Ogmore. He is a collieryman (below) and the Colliery APR Warden
