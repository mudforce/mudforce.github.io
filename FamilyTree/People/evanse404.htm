Evan Evans Biography

Evan Evans was born c.1845 (Q1 1846 MT 26 423) in Merthyr Tydfil, Glamorgan, Wales. His father was Evan Evans nd his mother was Ann Lovet.

Evan Evans married Martha Williams on 12th November 1865 in Zoar Chapel Merthyr Tydfil. He was a 21-year-old coalminer. She was a 21 yr old. Both were resident in Abercanaid. His father was Evan Evans, a coalminer. Her father was Lewis Williams, a coalminer. The witnesses were Lewis Williams and Thomas Evans, presumably his brother. (GRO Ref 11a 661).
