James Lewis Biography

Born 1838 and baptised 21 February 1838 in St Cadog Church, Llangadog, Carmarthenshire, Wales, being the son of Thomas and Elizabeth Lewis of Waunystrad Meiras as determined by the 1881 census.

Married Rachel Isaac on 12 September 1862 at Llanwrda Parish Church, Carmarthen.
