Clifford Leonard Dudden

Born: 26 October 1889 and baptised 17 November 1889 in Croscombe, Somerset
Died: Q3 1976 23 1039 Mendip District

Clifford's Family
Spouse 1: Ethel A Snelling married Q4 1911 in Shepton Mallet District (5c 973). She died 1932
Children: George P Dudden, Edith M Dudden, Olive A Dudden, Leonard F Dudden, Emily M Dudden, Elsie I Dudden, Dorothy V Dudden

Spouse 2: Violet Mudford married 1 September 1945 in Wells Register Office.

In the 1939 Register, Clifford was a widowed road foreman, in 6 Orchard Cottages, Long Street, Thrupe, Shepton Mallet RD, Somerset, England, liviing with three of his children.
