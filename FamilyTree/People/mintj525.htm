Jonathan Minty Biography

Jonathan Minty was born in 1785 and baptised 8th May 1785 in Hemington, Somerset, England. His parents were Roger & Betty Minty.

Jonathan Minty married Dorcas Silcox of Dunkerton, Wellow, Somerset, England by banns on 13 Apr 1815 at Foxcote, Somerset, England. Witnesses Betty Smith and Cornelius Rossiter. Page 1 Entry 1 Foxcote PRs.

They had 5 children:- James Minty, George Minty, Eliza Minty, Hester Minty and Mary Ann Minty
