George Mudford Biography

George was christened on 8th October 1854 at Midsomer Norton Somerset. He married Cecilia Laura Elford on 27th August 1885. George worked as a farm labourer for Maurice Hole at Chilcompton. Records show his places of residence as: 1889 4 Clapton Lane, Midsomer Norton, 1891 Rackfernell Rd, Midsomer Norton, 1901 68 High Street, Midsomer Norton, 1908 Stone's Cross, Midsomer Norton.

Children of George & Cecilia:

Eliza Kate Mudford, Louisa Alice Mudford, Elizabeth Laura Mudford, Herbert George Mudford, Mabel Esther Mudford, Eveline Ruby Mudford, Leonard John Mudford, Edgar Howard Mudford, Bertram James Mudford, Florence Irene Mudford, Edith Olive Mudford
