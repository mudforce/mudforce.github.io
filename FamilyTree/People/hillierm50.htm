Mary Jane Hillier Biography

Mary Jane Hillier was born 28th March 1865 in Kilmersdon Registration District in the County of Somerset. Her father was Tradue Hillier (yet another version of the spelling!) a coal miner. Her mother was Eliza Hillier formerly Maggs. Her mother on the 21st April 1865 registered her birth.

She married Joseph William Mudford on 17th October 1882 in the Parish Church of Midsomer Norton in the County of Somerset. He was aged 30 and a labourer living in Midsomer Norton. He didn't sign his name but made his mark. She was supposedly aged 19 and resident in Midsomer Norton. She signed her name. His father was Thomas Mudford, a labourer and her father, although at first glance is looks like Freddie, was in fact Treddue Hillier, a miner. This looks like the source of the Tredew forename given to one of their sons, although it is yet another spelling. The witnesses were George and Martha Hillier.

She and Joseph William had 6 children :- Arthur Thomas Mudford 18th Dec 1885 - 2nd Apr 1958, Joseph William Mudford born 12th Jul 1888, Ethel Maud Mudford born 10 Sep 1890, George Tredew Mudford 14th Dec 1892 - 15th Mar 1915, Mary Mudford born 29th Mar 1895, Mabel Mudford 17th Nov 1896 (4 months after J W’s death)- 4th Mar 1988

Subsequent children found to be hers: Edward Short Mudford 29th Mar 1898 - 18th Mar 1920 No father’s name on BC, Leslie Mudford born 1901 “Father’s Name” Joseph William Mudford (dead) on MC, Violet Mudford born 23 Jan 1905 No dad on BC. MC father George Mudford dead and Emily Mudford born 17-Dec-1900 Joseph William Mudford (deceased)on BC.

Marriage details found for 9th May 1908 at the Holy Trinity Church in the Parish of Coleford. George Reinford Forchen, bachelor 35 years old, a blacksmith of Highbury, Coleford, Father George Reinford Forchen (deceased) a horse doctor and Mary Jane Mudford, widow 40 years old of Highbury, Coleford, Father Tredhue Hillier a miner. Witnesses Henry and Emma Pike.

Subsequent child - Born 29 11 1909 Coleford, Somerset, England, Kenturia Evendine Forchan. Bert Willmott is her son. George's place of birth in the 1911 Census is given as Jamaica. He was in Shepton Mallet Prison doing 6 months with hard labour for unlawful wounding.

She died on 20th May 1923 in the Fry's Well, Midsomer Norton, Somerset, England. Her death was registered by E Pike her sister who was present at the death. She died on Schirrhosis of the liver, Bronchitis and Emphysyma
