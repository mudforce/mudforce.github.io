Mary Jane Hillier Biography

Mary Jane Hillier was born 28 March 1865 in Kilmersdon Registration District in the County of Somerset. Her father was Tradue Hillier (yet another version of the spelling!) a coal miner. Her mother was Eliza Hillier formerly Maggs. Her mother, on 21 April 1865 registered her birth.

She married Joseph William Mudford on 17 October 1882 in the Parish Church of Midsomer Norton in the County of Somerset. He was aged 30 and a labourer living in Midsomer Norton. He didn't sign his name but made his mark. She was supposedly aged 19 and resident in Midsomer Norton. She signed her name. His father was Thomas Mudford, a labourer and her father, although at first glance is looks like Freddie, was in fact Treddue Hillier, a miner. This looks like the source of the Tredew forename given to one of their sons, although it is yet another spelling. The witnesses were George and Martha Hillier.

She and Joseph William had 6 children :- Arthur Thomas Mudford, Joseph William Mudford, Ethel Maud Mudford, George Tredew Mudford, Mary Mudford, and Mabel Mudford born 4 months after J W's death).

Subsequent children found to be hers: Edward Short Mudford, No father's name on BC; Emily Mudford, Joseph William Mudford (deceased) on BC; Leslie Mudford Joseph William Mudford (dead) on BC, and Violet Mudford, No dad on BC.

Marriage details found for 9 May 1908 at the Holy Trinity Church in the Parish of Coleford. George Reinford Forchen, bachelor 35 years old, a blacksmith of Highbury, Coleford, Father George Reinford Forchen (deceased), a horse doctor and Mary Jane Mudford, widow 40 years old of Highbury, Coleford, Father Tredhue Hillier a miner. Witnesses Henry and Emma Pike.

Subsequent child - Born 29 November 1909 Coleford, Somerset, England, Kenturia Evendine Forchan. Bert Willmott is her son. George's place of birth in the 1911 Census is given as Jamaica. He was in Shepton Mallet Prison doing 6 months with hard labour for unlawful wounding.

She died on 20th May 1923 in the Fry's Well, Midsomer Norton, Somerset, England. Her death was registered by E Pike her sister who was present at the death. She died on Schirrhosis of the liver, Bronchitis and Emphysyma

In the 1921 Census Mary Jane (transcribed as Torchan) is living in Fry's Well, Chilcompton, Somerset, England with her son Leslie, her daughter now going as Katrina, her grandson William (Ehel Maud's illegitimate son), her married daughter Mary's Dennett family, including her illegitimate son, Thomas.
