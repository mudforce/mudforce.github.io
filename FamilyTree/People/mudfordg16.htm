Gordon Thomas Hillier Mudford Biography

Gordon Thomas Hillier Mudford was born 22nd April 1931 in Barry, Glamorgan, Wales. He worked as a middle manager with British Gas. He married Brenda Allen (born 19th May 1933) in All Saints Church, Barry, Glamorgan, Wales on 20th July 1957.

They had two sons. Lawrence and Haydn Mudford.

Lawrence (born 4th September 1958 in Barry, Glamorgan, Wales) qualified as a dentist and married Janet Hancock (born 24th September 1960) in St Mary's RC Church Crowborough, Sussex, England on 7th August 1982. She had a degree in Nursing. They’ve had 4 kids and currently live in Sussex.
