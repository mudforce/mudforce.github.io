Elizabeth Price Biography

Elizabeth Price was born in 1862 in Merthyr Tydfil, Glamorgan, Wales. She married David John (born 1862) in Q3 1882 in Merthyr Tydfil district. They had seven children, Benjamin (born 1884), Philip (born 1886), Miriam (born 1887, William (born 1890), Mary Elizabeth (born 1892), Maggie Ann (born 1893) and Eunice (born 1896).
