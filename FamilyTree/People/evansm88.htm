Joan Parte married Gordon Fitzgerald on 26th December 1949 in St. Peters Church, Abercanaid, Glamorgan, Wales. Two daughters Clare Fitzgerald (born Feb 1951 in London, England) and Hilary Fitzgerald (born 1954 in Abercanaid, Glamorgan, Wales).

A hysterectomy in 1946 triggered massive depression - hormone deficiency. Mum told the tale that she attempted suicide initially by gassing herself For her second attempt, she was found on the beach at Port Eynon on the Gower peninsula after taking pills.

Her death certificate however says she died in Troedyrafon, Alexander Place, Abercanaid. Cause of death was coal gas poisining accidently caused while dusting room. Perhaps the first attempt was successful after all or the second attempt was hushed up!!
