Gertrude (Gert) Butt (BK 84)
 Born 9 December 1900 in Stoke Lane, Somerset. In the 1939 Register she was a domestic servant in The Rectory, Templecombe, Wincanton, Somerset. She never married. She died 17 January 1984 in Shepton Mallet, Somerset, England.

Rowland Butt(BK 85)
 Born 11 April 1902 in West Cranmore, nr. Shepton Mallet, Somerset, England. He married Bertha I Wheeler (Rene) (BK 87), in Q1 1937 in Wells District, Somerset. In the 1939 Register, Rowland, a roadstone quarry hewer, and Irene were in 35 Waterlip Cramme, Shepton Mallet, Somerset, England. They had one son Michael Sidney Butt (BK 823) (who must be the closed record in 1939). He married Jean Reakes (BK 824). Rowland died Q3 1959 in Wells District.

George Reginald Butt (BK 86)
Born 1898 and baptised 9 April 1898 in Stoke St Michael, Somerset, England. He married Alma Witridge (BK 328) in Q2 1930 in Cranmore, nr Shepton Mallet. Had one child Margaret A Butt BK 329) married Len Bailey (BK 330)

Mr. G. R. Butt
The funeral took place on Thursday last at Evercreech Parish Church of Mr. George Reginald Butt, of 12,Southwood, Evercreech, who died the previous Sunday aged 64. He worked for some time for a case works at Bruton and later at the Evercreech brick works. For 18 years he worked on the road roller for Barnes Bros. of Trowbridge and for the last five years was employed as a case repairer at Messes. Showerings. He married Miss A. Witridge at Cranmore Parish in 1930 and had been living at Southwood for the past 11 years. He leaves a wife and one daughter.

The funeral service was conducted by the Vicar of Evercreech, the Rev. A.C.I. Selliack. Mourners were Mrs. R. Butt (wife);(BK 328) Mr. and Mrs. L. Bailey (daughter and son-in-law) (BK 329 and 330); Miss. G. Butt (sister) (BK 84); Mrs. R. Butt (sister-in-law) (BK 87); Mr. and Mrs. A. Kerton, and Mrs. W. Clarke, Mr. J. Clarke (also representing his wife) and Mr. P. Clarke (also representing his wife) (brothers-in-law and sister-in-law); Mr. M. Butt (BK 823) (also representing his wife and Mr. S. Clarke (nephews); Mrs. S. Harwood (also representing her husband) (friend); Mrs. C. Cable was unable to attend.

At the church were Mrs. Carpenter, Mrs. R. Bailey, Mrs H. Cox (representing Mr. H. Cox and John and Mr. and Mrs. Harwood), Mr. M. AIllen (representing Mrs. Allen), Mr. F. Clark, Mr. C. Adam (representing Showerings factory staff) , Mr. King (representing C.R. Society).

Florial tributes were from : Family cross from Alma, Len and Margaret; Gertie; Rene, Michael and Jean; Mabel, Arthur and family; Rose, Wilf and children; Clara, Jack and Dennis; Rhoda, Peter and Linda; Mr. And Mrs. Harwood, edie and Mrs. Harwood (Castle Cary); Mr. And Mrs. Bailey and Jack; Mr. And Mrs. Viney, Elaine, Rudi and Jim; Cis and family; Great nieces and nephews; Jane and Nettie; Mrs. Cable and Ivor; Mrs. Brine; John Kerton,Colin and Peter; S. Funeral arrangements were in the hands of Mr. A.R.W. Connock, Shepton Mallet.
