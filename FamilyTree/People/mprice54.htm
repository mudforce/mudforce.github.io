Mary Price Biography

Born 15th October 1871 at 17 The Square Pentrebach, Merthyr. Father Philip Price, Mother Mary Davies. Birth registered on 16th November 1871 by her father Philip Price. Occupation at time, Baller.

Married Evan Lloyd on 23rd June 1894 in Salem Chapel, Thomas Town, in the district of Merthyr Tydfil in the Counties of Glamorgan & Brecon. Evan Lloyd described as Colliery Timberman and address given as 32 Church Street Pentrebach. No father information given.

Mary Price address given as 20 The Triangle, Pentrebach. Father was Philip Price with a profession of Colliery Repairer. Witnesses were Philip Price (Brides Father), Catherine Price (Brides Sister) and Daniel Davies (relative of the Brides Mother, maybe her Uncle?)

Ann Price has no occupation because she lost her right arm age 12 in an accident crossing the railway line. She never married.

Margaret Price married a clergyman and had a son, Oswald.

Catherine Price married and had two sons, Elfed and Pethion. Elfed was a tram/bus driver on the route to Cefn cemetery. The family always used to wait for his tram to arrive because he never let them pay.

The Davies family are relatives of Mary Price Senior who was nee Davies, although this is a red herring. Dinah is another Price daughter known as Sarah in the 1881 census. Don’t know how they all fitted in as The Triangle houses were all two up/two down.
