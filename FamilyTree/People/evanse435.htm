Evan Evans Biography

Evan Evans was born 1812 and baptised 9th February 1812 in St Tydfil the Martyr Parish Church, Merthyr Tydfil, Glamorgan, Wales. His father was Evan Evans and his mother Elizabeth.

He married Ann Lovett by Banns in St. Tydfil the Martyr Parish Church, Merthyr Tydfil, Glamorgan on 9th January 1832. Both Evan Evans and Anne Lovett signed the register with a 'X' by mark.

Witnesses were David or Daniel Ap Lewis and William Richards. Banns called on Sunday 25th December 1831 and the 1st and 8th January 1832. There were no addresses or names of parent's on the documents.
