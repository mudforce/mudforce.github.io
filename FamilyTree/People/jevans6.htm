John Evans Biography

John Evans was born 17th November 1866 at 2 Pond Row, Abercanaid, nr Merthyr Tydfil. His father was Evan Evans, a coalminer and his mother was Martha Evans, formerly Williams. Evan Evans registered the birth on 29th December 1866. The address was 2 Pond Row, Abercanaid, Merthyr Tydfil, Glamorgan, Wales.

The last two are shown as boarders. They lived at her home after they were married before they had kids. At this point they were newly weds because John Evans and Margaret Walters were married on 21st February 1891 in Zoar Chapel, Merthyr Tydfil, (GRO reference number 11a 882). He was 24 years old, a coalminer from 52 Nightingale Street, Abercanaid. His father was Evan Evans, a coalminer. She was 25 years old from 42 Nightingale Street, Abercanaid. Her father was David Walters, a colliery repairer. The witnesses were Evan Evans (who made his mark) and David Walters.

In 1900 when DB was born, his profession was given as Coal miner. In 1929, when DB got married, his profession was given as Colliery lamp man. Had an accident at work that meant he couldn't go to loo without inserting a catheter, which is why he got a job as a colliery lamp man. Walked with 2 sticks. Had their rented house sold from under them to a chapel deacon so moved into Bryn Hyfred. Aunt Liz came home every day from work and got his lunch. Mum went to see him if no one else was at home. She remembers him as a sweet and gentle man.

. He died at "Trigfan", Chapel Street, Abercanaid on 5th June 1945 as written on his gravestone in Cefn Cemetery (on hill over other side of cemetery near the trees).
