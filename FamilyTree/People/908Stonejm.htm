Joseph Matthias Stone

Born: 24 February 1854 and baptised 26 March 1854 in St Peter and Paul, Shepton Mallet, Somerset, England
Died: 19 November 1917 in West Shepton, Shepton Mallet, Somerset, England. Probate Wells 29/01/1918 to Mary Rose Widow £1,862 2s 7d

There is a record of passage, cabin class, on the Cunard ship SS Albania, from Liverpool to Montreal, Canada, of Mary Stone, a 53 year old housewife, and Norah Stone, an 18 year old domestic on 19 April 1922, ultimate destination USA.
