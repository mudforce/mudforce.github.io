Leslie Mudford Biography

He was born on 18th February 1902 in Kennels, Stratton on the Fosse, Shepton Mallet, England. However his birth certificate is for a girl named Bessie. There is a note on the certificate which reads: In Entry No 375 Column 2 for "Bessie" read "Leslie" and in Column 3 for "Girl" read "Boy". Corrected on the 23rd November 1926 by me A. E. Nalder Superintendent Registrar on production of Statutory Declarations made by Sarah Cornick and Mary Dennett".

Sarah Cornick (nee Hillier) is his aunt and Mary Dennett (nee Mudford) is his sister.

He married Lilian Florence Lye, aged 30 on 1st October 1927 in Christ Church, Parish of Downside. His father was Joseph Mudford (deceased). Her father was Alfred Lye, a labourer. Witnesses were Edmund Thomas Dennett and Alfred Fear. She was a sponsor of Dad at his baptism.
