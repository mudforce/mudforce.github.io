Margaret Bess Price Biography

Born 1868 in Merthyr Tydfil, Glamorgan, Wales. Married James Pethian Davies, (born 1861 in Cardigan, Wales) in Merthyr Tydfil district Q1 1894 (11a 836), the local minister. Had one child, Oswald Davies, who was known as Ossie. He was born Q2 1895 (11a 246) in Haverfordwest, Pembrokeshire, Wales.
