Elizabeth Kertion Biography

She was born in 1836 in Chelynch, near Doulting, just outside Shepton Mallet, Somerset, England and baptised on 12th June 1836 in Doulting. Her father was Robert Kerton, a coal carrier. Her mother was Matilda Wilcox.

She married Thomas Francis on 24th January 1864 in St. Mary’s Church in the Parish of Bridgewater. She was a 24 yr old servant. He was a 23 yr old engine driver. Her Father was Robert Kertion, a labourer. His father was Jonas Francis, a labourer. The witnesses were John and Fanny Duck. (GRO ref 5C 629.)

Thomas Francis is living with his daughter in law in the 1901 Census as she died on 20th October 1897 in 80 Bath Road, Bridgewater, Somerset, England of heart disease
