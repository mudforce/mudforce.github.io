Joseph William Mudford Biography

Born 12th July 1888 in Midsomer Norton in the registration district of Clutton in the sub district of Midsomer Norton. His father was Joseph Mudford, a farm labourer. His mother was Mary Mudford, formerly Hillier. Mary Jane Mudford registered his birth on 21st August 1888.

His first wife was Florence Ann Amberry known as Florrie. They married in Tynewydd Road Congregational Church in Barry on 19th August 1911. She worked as a cleaner at Llandough Hospital. He went through life known as Mutford not Mudford. His marriage is thus not included in Brian Mudford’s excellent book.

Alma and Vi were his children.

Violet married Haynes in 1938 (using the surname Mutford). She married Jack Lyons in 1946 and had two children, Sylvia and Anthony.

His second wife wad Selina Harvey. They married Q3 1938 in Barry, Glamorgan, Wales (E. Glamorgan 11a 2163). He died Q4 1951 in Barry, Glamorgan, Wales (E.Glamorgan 8b 302)
