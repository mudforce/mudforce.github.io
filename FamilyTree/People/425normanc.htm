St. Mary's Church, Cannington and St. Martin's Church Fiddington are both in the Cannington Hundred and seen interchangable for family baptisms and marriages. A search of both those parish records can find only one marriage of a Thomas Norman and Sarah. On 5 July 1802 Thomas Norman married Sarah Whiting in St. Martins, Fiddington.
