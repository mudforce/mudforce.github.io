Charles George Francis

Born: Q1 1872 (Bridgewater 5c 445) and baptised 26 February 1872 in St. John's, Bridgewater, Somerset, England

Charles George's Heritage
Parents: Thomas Francis, Elizabeth Kertion
Siblings: Charlotte Matilda Francis, Clara Bessie Francis, Emma Jane Francis, Jonas John Francis

In 1898, Charles was listed as an Able Seaman working on the sail vessel Malfilatre, official number 67231, registered at the port of Bridgewater. In December 1913 he was registered as having a Merchant Seaman discharge in Cardiff. He was described as 5 foot 6, blue eyes, brown hair, fresh complexion and distinguishing marks of a dot on the back of both hands. Same again in Poplar March 1922, with a reference to the SS Dunvegan Casle, an Merchant ship scrapped in 1923, after service as a post ship and an armed merchant vessel in WWI.
