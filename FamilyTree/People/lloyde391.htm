Evan Lloyd Biography

Evan Lloyd was born 1804 and baptised 19 February 1804 in the parish church of Saint David & Saint Cyfelach, Llangyfelach. He was a son of William. From a review of the parish records he had a sister, Hannah Lloyd. The only possible marriage in the parish records are of William Lloyd and Ann Rees on 21 November 1789

He married Mary Rees in the parish church of Saint David & Saint Cyfelach, Llangyfelach, Glamorgan on 4 June 1836.

His eldest daughter Mary is shown as head of the household in the 1861 census so it is presumed he died in the period between the 1851 and 1861 census.
