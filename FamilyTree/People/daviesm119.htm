Mary Davies Biography

Mary Davies was born on 23rd May 1839 in Pentrebach, Glamorgan Wales. Her father was Ebenezer Davies, an iron puddler. Her mother was Elizabeth Davies, formerly Davies. Ebenezer registered the birth on 10th June 1839.

She married Philip Price on 27th August 1859 in Saron Chapel, Troedyrhiw, Merthyr Tydfil. GRO ref 11a 454. Neither of them signed their names. He was an ironballer, aged 21. She was a 20 years old spinster. Both had Pentrebach as their residence at the time of the marriage. His father was John Price, an iron puddler. Her father was Ebenezer Davies, an iron puddler. The witnesses were Ebenezer Davies, her father and Ann Thomas.

She died 22 March 1894 in Pentrebach and was buried in Saron Chapel Graveyard, Troedyrhiw.
