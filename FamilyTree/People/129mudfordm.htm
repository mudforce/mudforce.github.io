The 1851 Census for Great Field, Middle Chinnock, Yeovil, Somerset shows Joseph Mudford, widower, aged 57, hand loom weaver of sailcloth living with his son William, aged 16, daughter Mary Ann, 13, glover and two grandchildren: James Mudford, 16 born Middle Chinnock, and Hester, 7 born at Midsomer Norton.

In the 1861 Census Mary Ann Langdon was living with her husband Adam in 5 Higher Street, Middle Chinnock, Somerset, three doors away from her father Joseph Mudford in 8 Higher Street.
