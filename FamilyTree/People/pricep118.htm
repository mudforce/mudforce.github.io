Philip Price Biography

Philip Price was born on 2nd September 1837 at Tai Bach,Merthyr Tydfil. His father was John Price, a puddler. His mother was Elizabeth Price, formerly Lewis, who registered the birth, 9th October 1837.

He married Mary Davies on 27th August 1859 in Saron Chapel, Troedyrhiw, Merthyr Tydfil. GRO ref 11a 454. Neither of them signed their names. He was an ironballer, aged 21. She was a 20 years old spinster. Both had Pentrebach as their residence at the time of the marriage. His father was John Price, an iron puddler. Her father was Ebenezer Davies, an iron puddler. The witnesses were Ebenezer Davies, her father and Ann Thomas.

He died 11th August 1905 and is buried in Saron Chapel Graveyard, Troedyrhiw, Glamorgan, Wales.
