Frederick Francis Mountstevens' birth was registered in 1888 as Frederick Francis. He gained the surname Mountstevens after his mother's subsequent marriage. His place of birth, Bath Road, was her place of residence at her wedding. Nellie Mountstevens is an illegitimate daugher of Charlotte. Both Nellie (Ellen) and Fredderick were claimed to be children of Charlotte's parents in earlier Census'. In the 1881 Census Charlotte was living not far from her parents, working as a domestic servant. Her sister Clara was doing the same.
