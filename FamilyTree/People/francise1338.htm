Clara Bessie Francis Biography

She was born in Bridgewater, Somerset on 1st April 1867. Her father was Thomas Francis and her mother was Elizabeth Francis, formerly Kertion.

She married George Habgood in Wimborne, Dorset in Q2 1892 (5a 541)

In the 1939 Register, she, George and daughter Margaret are at Woodland Lodge, Liskeard in Cornwall, He is a Pensioned Postman.
