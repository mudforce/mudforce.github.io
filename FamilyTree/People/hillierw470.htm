William Hillier Biography

William Hillier was born 1778 in Gillingham, Dorset, England.

He married Virtue Chivers (BK Tree 482) on 1st October 1799 at Babington, Somerset, England. Witnesses were Sarah Turner and John Berryman. They baptised 3 children at Kilmersdon: -

Samuel 22nd June 1806 (BK 483)
Grace 4th Sept 1808 (BK 484)
Virtue 5th December 1810 (BK 485)

Virtue Chivers was buried aged 30 on 5th December 1810. It seems she died in childbirth.

William Hillier, then a widower, married Ann Button (BK 471) on 29thMarch 1812 at Kilmersdon. The witnesses were Charles Plummer and William Green.

William & Anna Hillier baptised children at Kilmersdon as follows: -
James 15th August 1813 (BK 472)
Giles 18th April 1815 (BK 473)
George 30th August 1818 (BK 474)
Anna 28th August 1825 (BK 475)
Mary 13th June 1830 BK 476)
Tarduus 2nd March 1823 (BK 319). This last one is  thought to be a very strange version of Tredhue! FreeReg website has it as Tordsuw!

Anna died sometime before 1848.

William Hillyar, widower, married Esther (or Hestor) Moore (BK 486), widow, on 2nd January 1848 in Coleford, Somerset, England. His father was named John. Witnesses Benjamin Martin and George Padfield.
