Dinah Price Biography

Born 29 Mar 1866 in Abercanaid, Glamorgan, Wales.

She married Rees Davies on 20th July 1889 in Bethesda Chapel, Merthyr Tydfil, Glamorgan, Wales. Rees Davies was a 26 year old coal miner of 16 Chapel Street, Abercanaid. Her address was given as 20 Pentrebach Square, Merthyr Tydfil. Her father was Philip Price, a colliery timberman. His father was Thomas Davies, a colliery labourer. They had 1 son, Thomas Henry and 4 daughters, Mary Anne, Ceridwen, Gwendraeth and Miriam.
