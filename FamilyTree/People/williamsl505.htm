Lewis Williams Biography

Lewis Williams was born 1824 and baptised 6th June 1824 in St Tydfil the Martyr Parish Church, Merthyr Tydfil, Glamorgan, Wales. His father was John Williams and his mother Martha.

He married Cesiah Llewelyn 23rd March 1844 in the Parish Church, Merthyr Tydfil, Glamorgan, Wales (they were both classed as minors and had eight children:-

Martha Williams born 26.05.1845
William Williams born 1849
Margaret Williams born 1854
Lewis Williams born 1858
Kezia Williams born 1859
Mary Ann Williams born 1863
John Williams born 1867
Elizabeth Williams born 1869
He died on 10th May 1898 and was buried in Graig Cemetery, Abercanaid, Glamorgan, Wales.

Headstone inscription Graig Cemetary, Abercanaid

Er cof am LEWIS mab LEWIS a KEZZIA WILLIAMS a fu farw Ionawr 9 1870 yn 12 oed.
Hefyd KEZZIA, yr uchod, a fu farw Chwefror 21 1879 yn 52 oed.
Hefyd LEWIS WILLIAMS a fu farw Mai 10 1898 yn 74 oed.
