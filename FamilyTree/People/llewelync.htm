Cesiah Llewelyn Biography

Cesiah Llewelyn was born in 1827 and baptised at St. Tydfil Parish Church on 15th July 1827. She was the daughter of William Llewelyn, a miner and Margaret. She was the second Cesiah these two baptised, there having been an older girl who died at the age of 8 months.

Cesiahâ€™s siblings were: Margaret Llewelyn Born 1806, Ann Llewelyn Baptised 30th July 1809 Buried 27th September 1816, Elizabeth Llewelyn Baptised 17thMay 1812, Ann Llewelyn Baptised 9th August 1818, Tabitha Llewelyn Baptised 19thNovember 1820, Cesiah Llewelyn Baptised 28thSeptember 1823 Buried 9thDecember 1823, David Llewelyn Baptised 23rdJanuary 1825

She married Lewis Williams 23rd March 1844 in the Parish Church, Merthyr Tydfil, Glamorgan, Wales (they were both classed as minors) and had eight children:- Martha Williams born 26.05.1845, William Williams born 1849, Margaret Williams born 1854, Lewis Williams born 1858, Kezia Williams born 1859, Mary Ann Williams born 1863, John Williams born 1867, Elizabeth Williams born 1869

She died 21st February 1879 and was buried in Graig Cemetery, Abercanaid, Glamorgan, Wales.
