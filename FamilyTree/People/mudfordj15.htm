John William Francis Mudford Biography

John William Francis Mudford was born 17th November 1929 in Barry, Glamorgan, Wales. He was the spit image of his father. When I was younger, I wondered why Gran had a photo of Uncle John on her wall rather than any of her other sons. It turned out to be my Grandfather Tom who I never knew. He died before I was born.

He married Anne Hartland (born 7th February 1931) in St. Paul’s Church, Barry, Glamorgan, Wales on 5th March 1955.

John Mudford worked for BP Chemicals in Barry all his working life and was an active union member, shop steward and union official. He and Anne had one daughter Irene (born 18th July 1957 in Barry, Glamorgan, Wales), who married Mike Owens (born 20th August 1951), a postman on 25th March 1978 in Windsor Road Reformed Church, Barry, Glamorgan, Wales. They had 6 children. Sadly, the second born son died aged one.

The Owens children are:-
Gareth Edward Owens 28th December 1979
Rhys John Owens 10th May 1981 - 30th October 1982
Rhiannon Margaret Eva Owens 9th March 1983
Bethan Sarah Elizabeth Owens 8th May 1984
Aled Michael Owens 9th August 1985
Cerys Louise Anne Owens 11th December 1986
