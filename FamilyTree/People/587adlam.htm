Ann Tibbs/Tucker

Born: 1792 and baptised 15 January 1792 in St Matthew, Wookey, Somerset, England
Died: Q1 1865 in Wells, Somerset, 5c 478 and buried 25 March 1865 in St Matthew, Wookey, Somerset, England

Ann's Family
Spouse: James Adlam married 9-January-1812 in Wookey, Somerset, England
Children: Mary Adlam, James Adlam, John Adlam, Thomas Adlam, George Adlam, Anne Adlam, Joseph Adlam, Sarah Adlam, Elizabeth Adlam

Ann's Heritage
Parents: John Tucker, Mary Tibbs
Siblings: Thomas Tucker, Esther Tucker, John Tucker

Ann was born to Mary Tibbs, who was unmarried. The parish register recorded the birth as Base-born. Within two years Mary married John Tucker a widower. Hence the parish register for Ann's mariage records her surname as Tibs Otherwise Tucker. In the 1861 Census, she is stated to be a landed proprietress. There is a suspicion that this came from John Tucker who may or may not have been her biological father. He had been married before to Hester Cook who died in Sept 1793 just a few months before he remarried.
