Anna Hillier Biography

Born and baptised 28th August 1825 in Kilmersdon Parish Church, Somerset, England

Joseph Plummer (BK 477) married Anna Hellyar (sic), Under age, on 25 Dec 1843 at Coleford. His father Doctor Plummer, her father William. Witnesses Giles Hellier, William Denning. He was a coalminer employed at the Vobster Coal Works. She was a knitter.

1841 Census Lympeat, Kilmersdon. She seems to have become Hannah for this census.
