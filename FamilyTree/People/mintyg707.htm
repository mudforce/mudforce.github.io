George Minty Biography

Born circa 1817 and baptised 13 July 1817 in Foxcote, Somerset, England. He married Ann Purbens in Q4 1838 Frome, who was born 1813 in Stratton on the Fosse, and died 1897, buried 3 February 1897 in Hemington, Somerset, England.

Died 1898 and buried 9th December 1898 in Blessed Virgin Mary, Hemington, Somerset, England
