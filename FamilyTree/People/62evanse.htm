Elizabeth Evans

Born: 30-Mar-1903 in Abercanaid, Glamorgan, Wales
Died: Apr-1996 in Merthyr Tydfil, Glamorgan, Wales and buried in Llwydcoed Crematorium, Aberdare, Glamorgan, Wales.

Elizabeth's Heritage
Parents: John Evans, Margaret Walters
Siblings: Martha Ann Evans, David Brinley Evans

In 1936 went nursing training in Bristol (Got SRN). Was the district nurse pre NHS. 1p a week club in the village. She collected the money, which paid her wages. Lived opposite Gran and Grandpa in Alexander Place, Abercanaid, with her dog, Friskie.

In the 1939 Register in 58 Chapel Street, Abercanaid, were John Evans (Retired Widower), Elizabeth Evans (State Registered Nurse), Albert E Parte (Club Steward) and Martha A Parte (Club Stewardess)
