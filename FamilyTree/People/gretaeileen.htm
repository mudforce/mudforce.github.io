Greta Eileen Mudford

Born: 24th November 1918 in Laurieton, New South Wales, Australia
Died: 13th January 1992 in Marin County, California

Greta Eileen's Family
Spouse: Chester Judah (American) married in New South Wales, Australia, 1943

First arrived in San Francisco, California from Melboune, Australia in January 1945, on the SS Kanangoora.

Also recorded is an air passenger manifest entry in Seattle, Washington, USA on June 24th 1955. Travelling 1st class from Manila, Phillippines were Capt. Chester Judah, Mrs Greta Eileen Judah, Master Chester Judah Jr, Miss Eileen Judah and Master Raymond Henry Judah

Also recorded date of naturalisation, December 7th 1955
