Violet Mudford Biography

She was born on 23rd January 1905 in Highbury, Kilmersdon Somerset. No father’s name on birth certificate. Mother was Mary Jane Mudford formerly Hillier, a charwoman. She registered the birth by making her mark on 3rd March 1905.

She was married to Clifford Leonard Dudden on 1st September 1945 in Wells Register Office. She claimed her father was George Mudford, a deceased farm worker. His father was George Edward Dudden, a deceased coal merchant.
