Leonard John Mudford

Born: 08 June 1899 in 68 High Street, Midsomer Norton, Somerset, England and baptised 13 September 1899 in the Methodist Church, Midsomer Norton, Somerset, England
Died: March 1985 in Midsomer Norton, Somerset, England

Leonard John's Family
Spouse: Gertrude Maude Lamb married 27 December 1924 in Midsomer Norton, Somerset, England
