Inter Rail 1984 Day 13

Friday 14th September 1984

 Woke up at 5.30am when a bunch of people came and went and again at about 6.30am when we arrived in Timisoara which is the second largest Romanina city. Had to get up to let the hordes off. A couple of English lasses got on our train and we finally got ourselves some seats by intimidating a number of Congo types.
