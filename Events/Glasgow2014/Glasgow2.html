Glasgow 2014

Part 2

 Not having been to Glasgow and the surronding area before, and not being able to book into one lot of accommodation for the duration, mostly because of stupid prices, I turned this into a bit of a touring holiday trip. My first port of call was the Ardencaple Hotel and Bar, Rhu, which is just north of Helensburgh, at the base of Gare Loch. To get there I chose to go the inland route, which took me past the bottom end of Loch Lomond.
