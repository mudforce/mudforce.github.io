2009

April 2009

 Mum, Dad and I decided to go back to Madeira for a weeks holiday. Dad was now not able to walk very far, and indeed, travelled in a wheelchair, which we used to take him out for an evening for dinner. So, the Isabel or any hotels at the top of the hill were not a consideration. We chose instead, the Hotel Porto Santa Maria, which was at the far end of the promenade, by the fort, on the flat, with restaurants not far away from the front doors.
